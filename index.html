I am Melkamu Wako, a diligent Computer Science and Engineering student at Adama Science and Technology University (ASTU). My passion for technology spans across multiple domains, from competitive programming to full-stack development and cybersecurity.

My expertise in C++ has been honed through competitive programming on platforms like Codeforces and LeetCode, where I solve complex algorithmic problems efficiently. This foundation in Data Structures and Algorithms enables me to tackle real-world challenges with optimal solutions.
