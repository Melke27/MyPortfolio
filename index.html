About Me

I am Melkamu Wako, a diligent Computer Science and Engineering student at Adama Science and Technology University (ASTU). My passion for technology spans across multiple domains, from competitive programming to full-stack development and cybersecurity.

My expertise in C++ has been honed through competitive programming on platforms like Codeforces and LeetCode, where I solve complex algorithmic problems efficiently. This foundation in Data Structures and Algorithms enables me to tackle real-world challenges with optimal solutions.

In web development, I excel at creating responsive and user-friendly interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. My full-stack capabilities extend to backend development with Node.js, Java, and RESTful APIs, complemented by database expertise in MySQL, MongoDB, and SQLite.

Beyond academics, I'm actively involved in CSEC ASTU and Team Alpha Robotics ASTU. My contributions to the robotics team helped secure 2nd place at the 2025 African Robotics Championship, where I developed scratch code and software automation systems. I'm also engaged in university tech clubs and peer-learning initiatives, constantly seeking opportunities for collaborative growth.

My commitment to cybersecurity is demonstrated through ongoing training with Cisco Networking Academy, focusing on ethical hacking, network security, and cyber defense. This dedication to security complements my goal of becoming a safe, ethical, and effective technical professional.
