About Me

I'm Melkamu Wako, a passionate Computer Science and Engineering student at Adama Science and Technology University (ASTU). I specialize in front-end development, crafting clean, responsive, and user-friendly interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. While front-end is my focus, I've also learned full-stack development to better understand how complete systems work, including backend tools like Node.js and Java.

I'm an active competitive programmer, regularly solving problems on platforms like Codeforces and LeetCode to improve my algorithmic thinking and coding speed.

I'm currently working toward a specialization in cybersecurity, taking online courses to strengthen my skills in areas like ethical hacking, network security, and digital defense. This aligns with my goal of becoming a secure and responsible tech professional.

Beyond academics, I'm a proud member of Team Alpha Robotics ASTU, where I contributed to our team placing 2nd at the 2025 African Robotics Championship. I'm also involved in university tech clubs and community projects, where I support fellow students and grow through collaboration.

I'm committed to continuous learning and professional development (CPD), always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow as a developer and future cybersecurity expert.
